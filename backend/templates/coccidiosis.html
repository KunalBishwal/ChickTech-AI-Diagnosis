About Coccidiosis

Coccidiosis is a parasitic disease in poultry caused by protozoa. It is one of the most common and economically significant diseases affecting chickens worldwide.

Symptoms

  • Bloody or watery diarrhea
  • Weakness and weight loss
  • Poor feed conversion
  • High mortality in severe cases

Cure & Prevention

  • Use of anticoccidial drugs (under veterinary guidance)
  • Vaccination of chicks
  • Maintaining hygiene in poultry houses
  • Providing clean feed and water
